The Global Groundwater Information System (GGIS) is an interactive, web-based portal to groundwater-related information and knowledge.
The GGIS consists of several modules structured around various themes. Each module has its own map-based viewer with underlying database to allow storing and visualizing geospatial data in a systematic way.
